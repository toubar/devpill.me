Devpill.me, an introduction

Posted March 7, 2022 by dcbuilder.eth ‐ 1 min read

Devpill.me is a public good blockchain development guide which acts as a learning resource aggregator for everything a blockchain developer might need. There are specializations targeting a wide range of different skillsets (i.e. smart contracts, front end, cryptography) and different topics like DeFi, MEV, NFTs, and more. To read more about it visit the Mirror announcement.
